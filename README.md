## Objective
The **Healthy Eating Pyramid Game** is an interactive, browser-based educational tool designed to teach children about nutrition and the importance of a balanced diet. 

The main goal is to help players identify and categorize different food items into their correct nutritional groups within the Healthy Eating Pyramid:
1.  **Fats, Oil, Salt, Sugar** (Eat Least)
2.  **Protein** (Eat Moderately)
3.  **Vitamins & Minerals** (Eat Most)
4.  **Carbohydrates** (Eat Most)

## Features
-   **Interactive Drag & Drop**: Intuitive controls for children to sort over **30 different food items**.
-   **Immediate Feedback**: Audio and visual cues (checkmarks/shakes) reinforce learning.
-   **Dynamic Scoring**: Tracks progress and congratulates the player upon completion.
-   **Engaging Visuals**: Vibrant, color-coded generic 3D-style pyramid design.
-   **Shuffled Gameplay**: Food items are randomized each time for replayability.

## How to Play
1.  Open [`index.html`](https://feast0101.github.io/healthy-food-pyramid-game/) in your web browser.
2.  Drag a food item from the right-side grid.
3.  Drop it onto the correct colorful layer of the pyramid.
4.  Listen for the "pop" (correct) or "buzz" (incorrect)!
5.  Clear all items to win!
